About Us

Who We Are

The company, LEGO Travel, was established in the suburban town of Kuliyapitiya, Sri Lanka, in the year 1999. In 2007, having received IATA accreditation, the company embarked on a journey with a primary goal of providing reliable and quality travel options to many travelers from within the North Western region of the country.

The company soon expanded its presence to other cities and currently has the largest travel branch network in the country, with its main office located in Colombo. The continuous growth and success of the travel business in Sri Lanka inspired us to take our footprint overseas with the opening of our two offices in Milan and Verona (Italy), an office in Melbourne (Australia), followed by the establishment of the newest office in Dubai (UAE) in June 2022.
